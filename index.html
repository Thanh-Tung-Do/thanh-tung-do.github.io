About Me

I help organisations make smarter decisions with data.

With over six years of experience across FMCG and education, I specialise in translating messy, high-volume data into clear commercial insight. My work has spanned national trade marketing operations at British American Tobacco, key account planning at PepsiCo, and teaching advanced data science methods to postgraduate students at the University of Technology Sydney.

I hold a Master of Business Analytics from UTS and am comfortable across the full analytical workflow — from writing SQL and Python to building Power BI dashboards and presenting findings to senior leadership. I'm drawn to problems where rigorous analysis can shift strategy, not just describe it.
